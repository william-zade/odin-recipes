Borscht Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz.) package pork sausage
  • 3 medium beets
  • 3 carrrots, peeled and shredded
  • 3 medium baking potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 medium head of cabbage, cored and shredded
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 (6 oz.) can of tomato paste
  • 8 3/4 cups water, divided as needed
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon sugar, to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Crumble sausage into a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stire until no longer pink. Remove from the heat and set aside.
  2. Fill a large pot halfway with water (about 8 cups) and bring to boil
  3. Add sausage to pot, cover pot, and return to boil. Add beets and cook until they have lost their color. Add carrots and potatoes, simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
  4. Add cabbage and diced tomatoes to the pot.
  5. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and cook until tender. Stire in tomatoe paste and remaining 3/4 cup of water until well blended. Transfer to pot.
  6. Add garlic to the soup, cover, and turn off the heat. Let stand 5 minutes. Stir in sugar and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Ladle into serving bowls, garnish with a dollop of sour cream and a sprig of parlsey. Also good with dark rye bread.

Nutrition Facts

(per serving)

  • Calories: 257
  • Fat: 14g
  • Carbs: 24g
  • Protein: 10g
