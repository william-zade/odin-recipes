Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients
  • 9 Lasagna Noodles
  • 1-1/4 lbs bulk Italian Sausage
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 3 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 2 Cans (one 28 oz, one 15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 6 oz. Cans of Tomato paste
  • 2/3 cups water
  • 2-3 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 tablespoons plus 1/4 cup minced fresh parsely, divided
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 3/4 teaspoon fennel seed
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 1/4 teaspoon coarsely ground pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 15 oz. carton Ricotta Cheese
  • 4 cups shredded part-skim mozarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions
  1. Cook noodles accoridng to package directions; drain. Meanwhile, in a Dutch oven, cook sausage, beef, and onion over medium heat for 8-10 minutes or until meat is no longer pink, breaking meat up into crumbles. Add Garlic, cook 1 minute. Drain.
  2. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, 3 tablespoons parsley, basil, fennel, teaspoon salt, and pepper; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a small bowl mix egg, ricotta cheese, and remaining 1/4 cup of parsley and 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  4. Preheat oven to 375o. Spread 2 cups of meat sauce into an ungreased 13"x9" baking dish. Layer with 3 noodles and a third of the ricotta mixture. Sprinkle with 1 cup mozarella cheese and 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers twice. Top with remaining meat sauce and cheese (dish will be full).
  5. Bake covered for 25 minutes. Bake uncovered for an additional 25 minutes or until bubbly. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.

Nutrition Facts

1 piece:
  • 519 calories
  • 27g fat (13g saturated fat)
  • 109mg cholesterol
  • 1013mg sodium
  • 35g carbohydrates (10g sugars, 4g fiber)
  • 35g protein
